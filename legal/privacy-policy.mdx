# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2025-11-03_

This Privacy Policy explains how Decentralized Rights Protocol ("DRP") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with our websites, applications, smart contracts, APIs, and services (the "Services").

## 1. Data We Do Not Collect
DRP does not custodian wallets or private keys. We do not collect seed phrases or private keys.

## 2. Data We May Collect
- Wallet addresses and on-chain activity (public blockchain data)
- Diagnostic and performance metrics (e.g., analytics) with privacy-preserving methods
- Voluntary information you provide through forms or community channels

## 3. Cookies and Local Storage
We may use cookies or local storage to remember preferences (e.g., language and theme). You can control cookies via your browser settings.

## 4. How We Use Information
To operate, improve, secure, and localize the Services; to communicate with you; and to comply with legal obligations.

## 5. Sharing
We do not sell personal data. We may share limited data with service providers that help operate the Services, subject to appropriate safeguards.

## 6. Security
We implement reasonable safeguards. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

## 7. International Transfers
Your data may be processed in jurisdictions with different data protection laws.

## 8. Your Rights
Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete certain information.

## 9. Changes
We may update this Policy periodically. Material updates will be posted here with a new "Last updated" date.

## 10. Contact
Please reach out via the contact methods listed on our website.
