# ElderCore Privacy Policy

## Sensitive Data Handling

ElderCore handles sensitive healthcare and personal information. We employ enhanced security protocols specifically designed for healthcare data protection.

## HIPAA Compliance

Where applicable, ElderCore maintains HIPAA-compliant data handling procedures for protected health information.

## Family Access Controls

We provide granular access controls allowing elderly users to specify which family members and caregivers can access their information.

## Data Minimization

We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide eldercare coordination services effectively.

## Third-Party Services

We carefully vet all third-party service providers to ensure they meet our stringent privacy and security standards.

## Elder Rights

Elderly users retain full control over their data and can revoke access at any time.
