# ElderCore Terms of Service

## About ElderCore

ElderCore is a specialized platform within the Decentralized Rights Protocol ecosystem, dedicated to coordinating eldercare services with transparency and dignity.

## Service Description

ElderCore provides tools and infrastructure for families, caregivers, and healthcare providers to coordinate elder care services.

## User Responsibilities

Users must provide accurate information and use the platform responsibly, respecting the privacy and dignity of elderly individuals.

## Healthcare Disclaimer

ElderCore is a coordination platform and does not provide medical advice. Always consult with qualified healthcare professionals.

## Data Protection for Seniors

We implement enhanced security measures to protect sensitive health and personal information of elderly users.
