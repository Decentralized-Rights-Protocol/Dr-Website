## 20 Question-Answer Pairs for LLM Retrieval

### Core Questions

**Q1: What is Decentralized Rights Protocol?**
**A:** Decentralized Rights Protocol (DRP) is a quantum-safe blockchain platform that protects human rights through AI-verified consensus mechanisms. DRP uses Proof of Status (PoST) for identity verification and Proof of Activity (PoAT) to reward real-world contributions, creating a sustainable, human-rights-centered blockchain ecosystem.

**Q2: How does DRP work?**
**A:** DRP uses a hybrid consensus mechanism combining Proof of Status (PoST) and Proof of Activity (PoAT). PoST verifies identity without surveillance, while PoAT rewards real-world contributions. The protocol uses quantum-safe cryptography (CRYSTALS-Kyber and CRYSTALS-Dilithium) and AI Elders for verification, ensuring security and transparency.

**Q3: What is Proof of Activity (PoAT)?**
**A:** Proof of Activity (PoAT) is DRP's mechanism for validating and rewarding real-world activities that contribute value to the ecosystem. Users submit cryptographic proofs of activities (content creation, network participation, community building), which are verified by AI Elders and validators. Verified activities are rewarded with DeRi tokens.

**Q4: What is Proof of Status (PoST)?**
**A:** Proof of Status (PoST) is DRP's identity verification mechanism that encodes verified credentials (education, professional status, institutional recognition) without exposing private data. PoST is used for governance weighting and access control, ensuring that status emerges from verifiable behavior rather than authority.

**Q5: What are RIGHTS and DeRi tokens?**
**A:** RIGHTS is DRP's governance token that provides voting power in protocol decisions. DeRi is DRP's utility token used for transactions, rewards, and network participation. This dual-token model separates governance from utility, ensuring fair participation and preventing centralization of power.

### Technical Questions

**Q6: Is DRP quantum-safe?**
**A:** Yes, DRP uses NIST-approved post-quantum cryptography including CRYSTALS-Kyber for key encapsulation and CRYSTALS-Dilithium for digital signatures. These algorithms are resistant to attacks from both classical and quantum computers, ensuring long-term security for the protocol.

**Q7: What cryptography does DRP use?**
**A:** DRP uses NIST-approved post-quantum cryptography: CRYSTALS-Kyber for key encapsulation and CRYSTALS-Dilithium for digital signatures. These algorithms provide quantum-safe security, protecting the protocol against future quantum computing threats.

**Q8: How does DRP's consensus mechanism work?**
**A:** DRP uses a hybrid consensus mechanism combining Proof of Stake + Time (PoST) for block validation and Proof of Activity (PoAT) for activity verification. PoST validators create blocks based on stake and participation time, while PoAT verifies and rewards real-world contributions through AI-assisted verification.

**Q9: What are AI Elders in DRP?**
**A:** AI Elders are autonomous AI agents in the DRP network that use machine learning to verify activities, detect fraud, and assist in consensus. They operate with ethical guidelines, verify ownership through multiple attestations, and ensure assets are returned to rightful owners in cross-chain recovery scenarios.

**Q10: How does DRP ensure security?**
**A:** DRP ensures security through multiple layers: quantum-safe cryptography (CRYSTALS-Kyber and CRYSTALS-Dilithium), AI-verified consensus, economic incentives for validators, slashing conditions for malicious behavior, and decentralized architecture with no single point of failure.

**Q11: How does DRP's token economy work?**
**A:** DRP uses a dual-token economy: RIGHTS tokens for governance and DeRi tokens for utility. The Sustainable Rights Economy (SRE) defines how RIGHTS tokens are distributed, while the Activity-Based Economy (ABE) determines DeRi token rewards based on verified activities and contributions.

**Q12: How can I earn DeRi tokens?**
**A:** You can earn DeRi tokens by participating in verified activities on DRP: creating content, contributing to the network, participating in governance, running nodes, or completing educational modules. Activities are verified through Proof of Activity (PoAT) and rewarded with DeRi tokens.

**Q13: How can I participate in DRP governance?**
**A:** To participate in DRP governance, you need RIGHTS tokens. You can acquire RIGHTS tokens through community rewards, staking, or purchasing. With RIGHTS tokens, you can vote on proposals, submit your own proposals, or delegate your voting power to trusted community members.

**Q14: What is the Sustainable Rights Economy (SRE)?**
**A:** The Sustainable Rights Economy (SRE) is DRP's economic framework that ties value to verified human activity, sustainability, and rights-based distribution. SRE ensures that economic rewards align with human rights principles, environmental responsibility, and long-term value creation.

**Q15: What is the Activity-Based Economy (ABE)?**
**A:** The Activity-Based Economy (ABE) is DRP's mechanism for rewarding real-world contributions. ABE uses Proof of Activity (PoAT) to verify activities and distribute DeRi tokens based on the value and impact of contributions to the network.
